Top Tourist Attractions

  • Tambulig River: A serene, crystal-clear river perfect for river tours and nature walks.
  • Mount Tambulig: A scenic hiking destination with panoramic views of the province.
  • Tambulig Mountain Spring Resort: Enjoy the beautiful coastline for swimming, relaxing, and water sports.

Festivals and Cultural Events

  • Pasko sa Tambulig: A Christmas festival showcasing local arts, crafts, and food.
  • Tambulig Harvest Festival: A celebration of the agricultural bounty of the region, with parades, dances, and traditional games.

Eco-Tourism and Sustainable Travel

Tambulig is committed to preserving its natural beauty. We encourage sustainable tourism practices, such as responsible waste disposal and supporting local businesses, to help protect our environment.
